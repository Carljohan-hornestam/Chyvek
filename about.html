About us

At Chyvek, we consist of six incredibly passionate individuals who works together in order to solve a big issue nowadays. More and more people become slackers. Workers get tired of their usual routines and students have no care for study. Business companies and school doesn’t know what to do and that’s why we created Chyvek! Our mission at Chyvek is to make these slackers realise how great their situation is. Our solution is to send them to bad jobs in order to reflect on themselves and be grateful for what they have. We also hold lectures at schools and companies to raise awareness of this issue. We have many satisfied customers and more to come!
